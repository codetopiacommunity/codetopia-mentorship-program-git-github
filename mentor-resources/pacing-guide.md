# Pacing Guide

Three pacing options. Pick the one that matches your cohort.

## Option 1: Casual — One Session Per Week

- Best for: working professionals, evenings only
- Commitment: 1–2 hrs/week
- Total: 8 weeks
- Capstones: weekend of week's end

## Option 2: Standard — Two Sessions Per Week (Recommended)

- Best for: bootcamp-adjacent, students with summer time
- Schedule:
  - Tue: Session 1
  - Thu: Session 2
  - Sat: Capstone work
- Commitment: 4–6 hrs/week
- Total: 8 weeks

## Option 3: Bootcamp Intensive

- Best for: full-time learners, sprint cohorts
- Schedule: 2 weeks of curriculum compressed into 1 calendar week
- Commitment: 25+ hrs/week
- Total: 4 weeks

## Switching mid-program

- Slow down if: >50% of cohort is missing capstone deadlines.
- Speed up if: capstones land early and discussions feel quiet.

## Buffer week

Build in a "review week" between Week 4 and Week 5 if you have it. The jump from local Git → collaborative GitHub is the steepest.
